real(kind=double), dimension(:), allocatable global_data::half_tide

Half tide, this is a complicated one. Ultimately it is for the calculation of the tide-integrated cross-shore distributions of drift and erosion. These use the static distribution of drift or erosion. These are shape functions (sf) sf_drift & sf_erode. These have abscissa increments (dx) of 1/40th of the breaker depth. Half tide is the tidal amplitude measured in these increments.
